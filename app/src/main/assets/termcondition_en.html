EU Mobile Money account conditions of use

1. Subject

The client subscribes to “EU Mobile Money” service of Express Union in order to: make funds transfers to an EU Mobile Money account, or to a natural person or legal entity, through his/her mobile phone, payable at EXPRESS UNION counters.

2. Conditions

To make money transfer through his/her mobile phone the client shall:
  • Permanently have provision in this account dedicated and lodged in the books of the institution.
  • Have a mobile phone and a number got from the mobile operators using GSM or CDMA installed in Cameroon.
  • Have Internet connexion or a mobile phone compatible with Java.

3. Provision account

  • The subscription for the EU Mobile Money service is free of charge.
  • The deposit made in the account is exclusively dedicated to the operations mentioned in the clause 5. a) point 4 and therefore is not for savings.
  • The related charges for all transactions made through mobile phone or via Internet are deduced automatically from the provision account.
  • The related charges for any remote deposit and withdrawal from an account (out of the branch where the account is found) or in the said branch are automatically deduced from the provision account.
  • The minimum amount of any deposit in the account is in CFA F 500 (ﬁve hundred)
  • The maximum amount for any remitance is 500,000 CFAF (ﬁve thousand) for an operation.

He shall also contact Express Union or the branch where the account is found in case he receives any call asking him to disclose his MPIN or IPIN code. He shall in no case communicate it to a third party.
    • After making an operation, the subscriber commits himself to keep the transaction's number as well as the transaction's code because they are compulsory for any payment.
    • He has the right to make the following transactions: deposit at a counter or via internet for withdrawal from his account and /or account deposit into another account or to a third party natural person or legal entity, bill payment …
    • The subscriber commits himself to follow the instructions found in the user guide that shall be given to him while subscribing.
    • The subscriber commits himself to make 01 (one) transaction at least over a period of 06 (six) months otherwise, the account shall be unilaterally locked by Express Union. The account shall be unlocked upon the client request.
